The Story Behind The Craft

TheFaberTale is led by Mr Akash Agrawal, an entrepreneur who has worked in the furniture business for more than seven years. He was fascinated by the beauty of craftsmanship in the furniture line. Inspired by these pieces of art, he discovered his passion towards these artefacts which create a space into a home. Early in his career, he became deeply interested in working with wood, and throughout his career, he has always been aiming for perfection. With a persistent determination and vision in his mind, he created TheFaberTale, as an ideal choice for furniture amongst the consumers.

Mr Agrawal is known for making stunning items that will last for a long time. He has a great sense of style and is a master craftsman. His hard work behind every piece of furniture has created a connection with the consumer and made a footprint in the industry. Driven by his passion he poured his heart into TheFaberTale. His exceptional work soon caught the eyes of consumers and design enthusiasts. For him success was never about profits, he believes that a happy consumer is the key to a successful business.

Ranjit Bhojiya, Co-founder

Ranjit Bhojiya, a visionary co-founder of The Faber Tale, brings a wealth of creativity and innovation to the world of epoxy resin furniture. With an eye for detail and a passion for design, Ranjit collaborates closely with artisans to craft unique, high-quality pieces that resonate with style and elegance. His dedication to pushing the boundaries of craftsmanship ensures that each creation from The Faber Tale not only meets but exceeds the expectations of discerning customers. Ranjit’s commitment to excellence and his ability to transform ideas into stunning reality make him an invaluable asset to The Faber Tale team.
